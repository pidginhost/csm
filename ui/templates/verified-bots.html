 Verified Bots

Allowlist legitimate crawlers and AI agents so they are not flagged or blocked. Each bot needs a User-Agent substring plus one verification method: a reverse-DNS suffix (search and SEO crawlers) or published IP ranges (AI agents that have no crawler reverse DNS).
Built-in bots (Googlebot, Bingbot, Applebot, DuckDuckBot, GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot and others) are always verified and need no entry here. Reverse-DNS suffixes must be registrable domains; shared-hosting suffixes and over-broad or non-public IP ranges are rejected so the list cannot become a bypass.
